Document List of Lek Ladki Yojana

  1. Applicant's Birth Certificate
  2. Family's Annual Income Certificate (should not exceed 1 lakh) from Tehsildar / competent authority
  3. Photocopy of the applicant's Aadhaar card (this is optional for the first girl child)
  4. Photocopy of the parent's Aadhaar card
  5. Photocopy of the first page of the bank passbook
  6. Ration card (photocopy of pink or yellow ration card)
  7. Photocopy of the Voter ID card (if the applicant is 18 years old, name should be on the voter's list)
  8. School certificate (Bonafide) if the applicant is currently studying
  9. Family planning operation certificate (if the application is for the second or subsequent girl child, this certificate is mandatory)
  10. Self-declaration by the final beneficiary
